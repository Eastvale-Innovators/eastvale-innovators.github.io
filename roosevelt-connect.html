Roosevelt Connect

A flexible scheduling and office hours platform for Eleanor Roosevelt High School — scan in, show up, get help.

Deployed AtEleanor Roosevelt HS
Status✓ Deployed
The Problem

What it solves

Managing office hours at a large high school is messy — paper sign-ups get lost, teachers can't easily pull in specific students, and there's no good way to track who showed. Roosevelt Connect replaces all of that with a clean digital system built around how the school actually works.

  • →Students scan their school ID to log attendance at office hours — no paper, no friction.
  • →Teachers can request specific students to attend — like a digital hall pass for academic support.
  • →Think Flextime at Irvine High — but built from scratch for Roosevelt.
The Approach

How we build it

Every feature maps to a real campus workflow — prototyped fast, tested with actual students and staff, and iterated until it's rock solid. Reliability and speed aren't goals, they're requirements.

What It Does

Key Features

🪪

ID Scan Check-In

Students scan their school ID to instantly log attendance at office hours — fast, accurate, zero paperwork.

📩

Teacher-Initiated Requests

Teachers can pull specific students into an office hour session directly through the app — no chasing people down in the hallway.

📅

Flexible Scheduling

Students can browse available office hour slots and sign themselves up — giving them ownership over when they get help.

📊

Attendance Tracking

Every check-in is logged automatically, giving teachers and staff a clear record without any manual data entry.
