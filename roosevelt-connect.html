Roosevelt Connect

Eleanor Roosevelt School App

Roosevelt Connect is a reliable software platform for school initiatives at Eleanor Roosevelt High School, built by students through iterative design, prototyping, and testing to support day-to-day campus workflows.

Overview

Project Overview

A practical campus software system shaped by feedback loops, reliability goals, and real school usage.

What It Solves

School initiatives need dependable software that staff and students can trust under daily workloads. Roosevelt Connect focuses on clarity, predictable behavior, and maintainable delivery so the platform continues to improve over time.

  • Transforms initiative ideas into production-ready workflows.
  • Prioritizes stable behavior across real-world usage scenarios.
  • Keeps iteration cycles fast through prototyping and testing.
