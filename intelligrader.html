What It Does

Project Overview

FRQ grading is slow, inconsistent, and often too vague to be useful. Intelligrader fixes that — paste in any FRQ response, get back a scored, rubric-aligned breakdown with specific feedback in seconds. It works across subjects, not just AP History.

  • →Grades any FRQ — the AI understands rubric structure across subjects.
  • →Feedback is specific enough to act on immediately, not just a score.
  • →Students can revise, resubmit, and actually track their own improvement.
  • →Teachers get time back — Intelligrader handles first-pass scoring so they can focus on coaching.
