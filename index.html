Who We Are

About Eastvale
Innovators

A student-led organization at Eleanor Roosevelt High School dedicated to bridging STEM pathways and solving real-world community challenges through innovative projects and internships. We aren't just a club, we're a community.

Meet the Teams →
🎯

Our Mission

To unite STEM pathways by providing opportunities through solving pressing community issues via projects and internships.

🔭

Our Vision

Students develop skills alongside peers and mentors, gaining real-world experience — from technical to leadership abilities.

💡

Our Impact

Empowering the next generation through hands-on experience in medicine, engineering, and computer science.

First major project — an EMR portal for virtual medical outreach. Built the architecture, established security standards, and learned how to ship real software.
