01 — Overview

Secure software for mission-driven care.

Virtual Medical Missions is building an electronic recordkeeping and operations platform that supports outreach, partnerships, and day-to-day care workflows.

What It Supports

Mission work depends on reliable systems for patient records, volunteer coordination, outreach campaigns, and fundraising operations. The platform brings those workflows together in a secure, scalable portal that can grow alongside the organization.

  • Electronic medical recordkeeping designed for outreach contexts
  • Partnerships, fundraising, and operations in one secure portal
  • Built for scalability and long-term mission growth
  • Security and privacy treated as first-class requirements
